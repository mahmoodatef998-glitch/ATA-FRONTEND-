========================================
   ملخص المتغيرات (9 متغيرات)
========================================

✅ DATABASE_URL                    (Connection Pooling)
✅ DIRECT_URL                      (Direct Connection)
✅ NEXTAUTH_SECRET                 (Authentication)
✅ NEXTAUTH_URL                    (⚠️ حدثه بعد Deploy)
✅ NODE_ENV                        (production)
✅ RBAC_ENABLED                    (Permissions System)
✅ NEXT_PUBLIC_RBAC_ENABLED        (Client-side RBAC)
✅ NEXT_PUBLIC_API_URL             (Backend URL)
✅ ALLOWED_ORIGINS                 (⚠️ حدثه بعد Deploy)

========================================
   خطوات بعد Deploy الأول
========================================

1. انسخ URL المشروع من Vercel
   مثال: https://ata-crm-frontend.vercel.app

2. حدث في Vercel:
   - NEXTAUTH_URL → URL المشروع
   - ALLOWED_ORIGINS → URL المشروع + Railway URL

3. حدث في Railway Backend:
   - CORS_ORIGIN → URL المشروع

4. Redeploy كلا المشروعين
